Our Story

From a Passionate Tech Team to Tanzania's Trusted ICT Partner

Maptech Company Ltd is a Dar es Salaam-based ICT solutions provider specialising in cybersecurity, networking, IT infrastructure, digital transformation, web development, graphic design, branding and system integration.

Founded with a clear mission to make high-quality technology accessible to every Tanzanian business, we have grown from a small team of developers into a full-service ICT company with certified engineers, creative designers and experienced trainers. Over 5+ years, we have delivered more than 150 projects and trained over 500 students.

Today, Maptech serves corporate clients, government institutions, NGOs and SMEs across Tanzania — providing innovative, reliable and secure technology solutions designed to meet the unique needs of businesses at every stage.
